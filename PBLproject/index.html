About Us

1. ITM is one of the best engineering college libraries among engineering college in the state. It started its function with a vision to serve the students and faculty with information needed for them. It mainly holds books related to engineering and allied subjects. The ITM established its library in the year 1997. The library collection is more than lakh volumes. The library has rich collection of Reference & Text Books, Dictionaries, Yearbooks, Encyclopedias, General books, and Fiction & novels. The library has also subscribing for National & International Journals, General & Technical Magazines.

2. Library has adopted open access system, and maintains OPAC for catering to the needs of the students and faculty.

3. Digital Library with multimedia facility (30 Systems) accesses the e-Journals, e-books, NPTEL videos and other Online education materials. Library is fully computerized using Soul Software. Students and staffs can access the library resources by sitting at home visiting the Online LMS.

To develop the institute into a centre of excellence in education, research, training and consultancy to the extent that it becomes a significant player in the technical and overall development of the country.
